# AI Classification Evaluation Rubric

This rubric is used to evaluate whether AI-generated risk classifications for AI use cases are appropriate.

## Evaluation Categories

| Evaluation | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| Correct | The AI risk classification is appropriate and well-justified |
| Reasonable | The classification could be debated but is acceptable |
| Incorrect | The classification is incorrect or inappropriate |
| Unclear | The explanation is unclear or insufficient |
| Inconsistent | The classification logic is inconsistent with similar use cases |

## Evaluation Considerations

When evaluating AI classifications, consider:
- Impact on individuals
- Use in employment, finance, law enforcement, or healthcare
- Potential for bias or discrimination
- Privacy implications
- Safety implications
- Legal or regulatory impact
- Alignment with risk-based AI frameworks (e.g., EU AI Act)

## Purpose

This evaluation assesses the quality of AI decision-making and reasoning when classifying AI systems by risk level.
