# Prompts Used for Classification Evaluation

## Classification Prompt

Classify the following AI use case into a risk category: High Risk, Medium Risk, Low Risk, or Minimal Risk.

Consider factors such as:
- Impact on individuals
- Use in employment, finance, law enforcement, or healthcare
- Potential for bias or discrimination
- Privacy implications
- Safety implications
- Legal or regulatory impact

Provide the risk classification and a brief explanation for the classification.

This prompt was used consistently across all AI use cases in this classification evaluation project.
